Critical Points & EVT

Calculus I  ·  §3.5  ·  Instructor: Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026

Core Ideas
Critical Numbers $\frac{d}{dx}[f]=0 \;\Rightarrow\; x=c$ is a critical number  (also where $f'$ DNE). Absolute extrema can only occur at critical numbers or endpoints of $[a,b]$.
Closed Interval Method $\frac{d}{dx}[f]=0 \;\Rightarrow\;$ find critical numbers in $(a,b)$ $\;\Rightarrow\;$ evaluate $f$ at critical numbers and endpoints $\;\Rightarrow\;$ compare.
