Core Ideas
FTC Part 2 — Evaluation Theorem If $F$ is any antiderivative of $f$ on $[a,b]$, then $$\int_a^b f(x)\,dx = F(b) - F(a)$$ Find the antiderivative, evaluate at both endpoints, subtract bottom from top.
FTC Part 1 — Derivative of an Integral $$\frac{d}{dx}\int_a^x f(t)\,dt = f(x)$$ With chain rule: $\dfrac{d}{dx}\displaystyle\int_a^{g(x)} f(t)\,dt = f(g(x))\cdot g'(x)$
