Concavity & 2nd Derivative Test

Calculus I  ·  §3.6.5  ·  Instructor: Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026

Core Ideas
Concavity $\frac{d^2}{dx^2}[f]>0 \;\Rightarrow\; f$ concave up ∪  •  $\frac{d^2}{dx^2}[f]<0 \;\Rightarrow\; f$ concave down ∩. Inflection point: where $f''$ changes sign. Find by setting $f''=0$ and checking the sign change.
Second Derivative Test At $x=c$ where $f'(c)=0$:   $f''(c)>0\;\Rightarrow\;$ local min  •  $f''(c)<0\;\Rightarrow\;$ local max  •  $f''(c)=0\;\Rightarrow\;$ inconclusive (use FDT).
