Product & Quotient Rule

Calculus I  ·  §2.5  ·  Instructor: Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026

Core Ideas
Product Rule $$\frac{d}{dx}[f \cdot g] = f'g + fg'$$ "Derivative of first times second, plus first times derivative of second." Use when two factors cannot be combined into a single power first.
Quotient Rule $$\frac{d}{dx}\!\left[\frac{f}{g}\right] = \frac{f'g - fg'}{g^2}$$ "Low d-high minus high d-low, over low squared." Order in the numerator matters — swapping gives the wrong sign.
