Core Ideas
Strategy: Read the Structure Is there a product or quotient at the top level? Use that rule first. Does either factor involve a composite? Apply chain rule to it. Work outermost to innermost, one layer at a time.
Key Values for Evaluation $e^0=1$, $\ln(1)=0$, $\sin(0)=0$, $\cos(0)=1$ — evaluating at $x=0$ or $x=1$ turns most multi-rule expressions into clean integers.
