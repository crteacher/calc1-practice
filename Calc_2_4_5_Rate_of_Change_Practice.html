Rates of Change

Calculus I  ·  §2.4.5  ·  Instructor: Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026

Core Ideas
Position, Velocity, Acceleration $$v(t)=s'(t) \qquad a(t)=v'(t)=s''(t)$$ $v(t)>0$: moving forward.   $v(t)<0$: moving backward.   $v(t)=0$: at rest. Set $v(t)=0$ to find when an object reverses direction.
Marginal Cost If $C(x)$ = cost to produce $x$ units, then $C'(x)$ = marginal cost (approximate cost of one additional unit at production level $x$).
Practice Problems
