Core Ideas
Limit LawsIf $\lim f$ and $\lim g$ both exist: $\lim[f\pm g]=\lim f\pm\lim g$,   $\lim[fg]=(\lim f)(\lim g)$,   $\lim[f/g]=\lim f/\lim g$ (when $\lim g\ne0$).
0/0 strategyWhen direct substitution gives $0/0$: factor, rationalize, or expand — then cancel the offending factor and substitute.
